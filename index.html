Creature assignment

In this assignment we had to draw a creature and animate it. This assignment introduced us to the basic programming principles. Our creature had to have classes, animation and user-interaction. Additionally, we also had to use the concepts of abstraction, encapsulation and user interaction.

For my creature assignment I made a cat. The program displays five cats. All the cats can be moved to wherever you want, but not outside the canvas. When you move or click on a cat, the cat will change its position. It will seem like he is jumping in the air. By pressing any key, all the cats will jump at the same time. Each cat has a different height and distance it will travel before landing again. When a cat is jumping it will also be in its jumping position. The cats can ofcourse not bounce out of frame. If the cat hits one of the two sidewalls, he will bounce back. If the jump is too high, the cat will hit its head and bounce back down. Each cat also has its own butterfly. At the start of the program these butterflies will fly around the screen. When a butterfly hits the left ear of his owner cat, it will stay there. A butterfly can land on its owner ear by flying on it, or you can move the cat towards the butterfly. You don't know wich butterfly belongs to which cat, so you will just have to try until one sticks. Once sitting on the ear, a butterfly will calmly remain there while the cat is being moved. But watch out! If a cat jumps with a butterfly on its ear, it will scare the butterfly and it will fly away again.

Call center

This project was done during module 4 of the study programme of Creatve Technology. The module was called Art&Impact and the project was done in group of 7 people. The goal of the project was to convey a certain emotion using an interactive installation. At the start of the project two concpets were given to start the brain storming. For our project these were: Persona & Speakers.

The concept we came up with is the setting of a call center. With the classic little office booths and personal items on the desk. The goal of our project was to make the user feel overwhelmed at first, by overlapping call noises accumillating in decibells. After this, the user can unplug the wire of the phone and they will hear a high beep and they can plug back in to hear serenity noises. These are things like nature sounds or someone laughing in the background. The message that we wanted to convey was that it is okay to set your boundaries and remove yourself from the situation if it is getting to much for you.
